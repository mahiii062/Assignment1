Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is defined as the process of bringing about new ideas, methods, products, services, or solutions that have a significant positive impact and value.

Sustainability

"Sustain" means to last over time, so sustainability is the ability of something to last.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is defined as the basic physical systems of a business, region, or nation and often involves the production of public goods or production processes.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the degree of autonomy and self-determination in people and in communities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 is more than a new administration—it's a vision of a nation reborn from the ashes of its past mistakes, driven by a shared dream of unity, transparency, and progress. However, with this rebirth comes the need to carefully manage global perceptions

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation is the process of using digital technologies to improve a country's services, economy, and society. It involves rethinking how governments, businesses, and citizens interact with each other.

Green Energy

Green energy is energy that comes from renewable sources and has the greatest environmental benefit. It's also known as renewable energy.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure is a system of physical and digital structures that support a region, business, or nation. It includes the physical systems that produce goods and services, as well as the digital systems that support data collection and analysis.

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 11/01/2025 Time: 10:25 PM

Bangladesh has launched its second satellite, a significant step towards strengthening its space technology capabilities. This new satellite will enhance telecommunications, internet services, and disaster management. It aims to provide better connectivity in remote areas and support the nation’s digital transformation goals. This achievement marks a milestone in Bangladesh's pursuit of technological advancement and self-reliance in space communication.
